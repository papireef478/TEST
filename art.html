🖌️ Today's Art Project

Watercolor Landscapes

Materials needed: Watercolor paints, paper, water cup, brush

Steps:

  1. Sketch a simple landscape (mountains, trees, or sunset)
  2. Start with light washes of color
  3. Let layers dry before adding details
  4. Sign your masterpiece! 🎨

🎭 Art History Spotlight

Frida Kahlo (1907-1954)
