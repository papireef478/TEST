Quick Guide

This is the official quick guide for the San Junipero Department of Justice. It explains the basics of interacting with the DOJ, filing cases, and understanding your rights.

  • All legal matters must be filed through a DOJ Ticket in Discord.
  • Clerks are available to assist with paperwork and fees.
  • Court schedules are posted daily in #court-docket.
  • Evidence requires screenshots or video (Rule 12.6).
